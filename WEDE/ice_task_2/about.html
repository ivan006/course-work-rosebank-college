About Rosebank College

Rosebank College is a higher education institution in South Africa. It offers diplomas, degrees, and higher certificates in fields like IT, Business, Education, and more.

Key Terms

Higher Certificate
An entry-level qualification focusing on foundational skills in a specific field, usually completed in 1 year.
Diploma
A qualification that provides both theoretical and practical training in a chosen field, generally over 3 years.
Bachelor's Degree
An undergraduate academic degree awarded for a course of study lasting 3 to 4 years.
Field of Study
The main area or discipline in which a qualification is focused, such as IT, Business, or Education.
Subjects Required
The school subjects a student must have completed in order to be admitted into a specific qualification.
